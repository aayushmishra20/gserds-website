Introduction

Welcome to GSERD-Empowering Rural India, Transforming Lives, Enriching Communities: We are a non-profit organization dedicated to uplifting the most vulnerable and underprivileged individuals in rural communities. Through comprehensive support, we unlock potential and foster lasting change, empowering rural individuals to become change-makers and drive positive impact.

Our mission is to empower rural communities to thrive through education, economic opportunities, healthcare, and inclusive growth, supporting entrepreneurship, skills development, and livelihood enhancement, fostering a more equitable society.

At GSERD, we amplify marginalized voices, cultivate leaders and change-makers, and drive systemic change and policy reforms.

About Us

Good Shepherd, Educational and Rural Development Society (GSERDS), a non-profit organization, has been dedicated to serving and improving the lives of rural Indians since 2000.

Our goal is to increase economic opportunities and social empowerment for underprivileged individuals in rural India by 2027 through comprehensive programs and strategic partnerships.

Key Focus Areas

Unlocking Potential: Our comprehensive approach empowers rural individuals to realize their aspirations, fostering innovative entrepreneurship, economic resilience, educational attainment, leadership development, and community engagement. By unlocking this potential, we catalyze transformative change, improving livelihoods and contributing to vibrant, self-sustaining communities.

Quality Education and Vocational training: Enhancing academic achievement, employability, and industry relevance through accessible education and skills development. Our NGO nurture disadvantaged individuals to break the cycle of poverty and secure a better livelihood.

Mentorship: Supporting individuals through support, coaching and networking to improve personal growth, leadership, and career development. Our mentorship programs connect beneficiaries with experienced professionals, promoting confidence, resilience, and informed decision-making.

Leadership Development: Cultivating capable and influential leaders through training, mentorship, and practical learning. Our programs equip individuals with essential skills, knowledge, and confidence to drive positive change and community impact.

Entrepreneurship Coaching: Empowering individuals and communities to achieve self-sufficient livelihoods and economic growth through sustainable entrepreneurship.

Financial Assistance: Providing vulnerable individuals and families with timely economic support to alleviate poverty and financial hardship. Our NGO offer accessible funding, grants, and resources to enhance financial stability, self-sufficiency, and overall well-being.

Healthcare: Improving access to quality medical services, promoting wellness, and addressing health disparities in vulnerable communities. Our NGO provide essential healthcare, screenings, treatments, and education to enhance overall health and well-being.

Core Objective

Poverty Alleviation: To empower rural communities in India through sustainable poverty alleviation initiatives, fostering financial freedom, self-reliance, and holistic development, thereby improving quality life and promoting equitable societal growth.

Education: To provide inclusive, high-quality formal education to rural communities in India, equipping individuals with knowledge, skills, and informed decision-making abilities, thereby contributing to India’s socio-economic growth, poverty alleviation, and sustainable development.

Entrepreneurship Coaching: To ignite entrepreneurial spirit among rural individuals, providing comprehensive support through financial assistant, coaching, mentoring, and resources, fostering innovative business growth, financial stability, and job creation, thereby transforming lives and contributing to India’s socio-economic advancement.

Rural Development: To empower rural India through health and wellness initiatives, rural industrialization, agriculture modernization, education and skill development, environmental sustainability, and community-led development, enhancing quality of life, economic growth, and social equity.
